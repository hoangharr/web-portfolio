3. Auxiliary Adverbs

Adverbs of degree or intensity function as auxiliary modifiers. In professional English, they elevate your language from basic descriptions to precise, impactful statements.

lightbulb

PRO-TIP: PRECISION

Avoid overusing "very." Instead, use specific auxiliary adverbs like "Consistently" to demonstrate reliability over time.
